Frequently asked questions

Where does DRIP ship from?

All DRIP orders ship from US warehouses. You won't see any Chinese text on the packaging — items arrive in clean, unbranded boxes ready to gift or keep.

How long does shipping take?

Standard delivery takes 3–8 business days within the United States. We don't offer international shipping at this time.

Is DRIP free shipping available?

Yes — orders over $50 ship free. Orders under $50 have a flat $4.99 shipping fee.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn and in original condition. Contact us and we'll send a prepaid return label.

Are DRIP products good quality?

Every product is hand-picked from vetted suppliers and quality-checked before shipping. Our average rating across all products is 4.8 stars from thousands of real buyers.

What sizes does DRIP carry?

Most clothing items run from S to 3XL. Size guides are shown on each product page. We carry inclusive sizing across all our women's and men's styles.
